01 · The Duo

Two brothers.
One unmistakable rhythm.

Berdan and Kadir Kalin are a Belgian-Turkish dance duo, born and raised in Belgium with roots in Erzincan. Brothers, not twins. A four-year age gap, and one unmistakable rhythm.

Their breakthrough came in 2020. During the pandemic Kadir filmed Berdan dancing at a family gathering and posted it to TikTok without telling him. The video went viral overnight. What began in a small bathroom and a family living room turned into a global audience of millions, with viral trends that travel far beyond their core community.

Today they work across seven accounts on TikTok, Instagram, YouTube and Snapchat, with a combined reach in the upper tier of European creator talent. A duo that brands and venues bring in to create moments people re-share, remix and remember.

04 · Trends They Started

Originators,
not amplifiers.

"Ashley, look at me"
Their interpretation of the “Look at me” meme became one of Instagram's most-replicated trends of the decade, peaking at 155M views and pulling in collabs from international names including chef CZN Burak.

“Yali Yali”
Their dance to the Romanian track Yali Yali helped re-launch the song into the European TikTok sphere and into the Balkan and MENA diaspora communities. A textbook case of breaking a track into new markets.

Barbershop Duet
The first format that crossed the 20M-view line on TikTok, and the format other creators still recreate. The recognisable visual: two brothers, mirror choreography, tight cuts.
